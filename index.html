In college I chose to explore my interests in science and majored in Biochemistry at North Carolina State University. I love everything about Biochemistry. The complexity, the knowledge of the inner mechanisms of life, and the difficulty. With this degree I have been able to work with infectious diseases such as HIV and Hepatitis C. However, I took one statistical programming class using SAS and my desire to code returned. Im grateful that I have a wonderful girlfriend that introduced me to this program and pushed me to pursue a new career path.
